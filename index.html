I will be starting a Ph.D. at the University of Waterloo with Profs. Kimon Fountoulakis and Aukosh Jagannath in fall 2020. Prior to this I was an M.Math. student with the Algorithms Group, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jeffrey Shallit. During my master's, I worked on algorithmic number theory, combinatorics on words and automatic theorem-proving. More recently, I'm interested in statistical problems on graphs.

Previously (2016-2018) I spent a wonderful couple of years at Microsoft in the Azure Compute ecosystem, working majorly on cloud services involving migration and replication of virtual & physical machines and datacenters. Before that I was a computer science major at IIT Jodhpur where I worked with Prof. Venkata Badarla on network and compute allocation in datacenters.

As an undergrad, I've been an avid sports programmer and participated in several competitions including ACM-ICPC, Code Jam, HackerCup, and contests on CodeForces, CodeChef and HackerRank. I've also played CTF style hacking contests including InCTF, Google CTF, Facebook CTF and others.
